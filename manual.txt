
Factions:
These are the different allegiances in the game world. Their relationship to each other can change over time, though towards the PCs will be most volatile.

'civilisation'
'wild'
'bandits'
'froblins'
'pc party' - only to be referenced by others. Player chooses interactions towards the others.

Encounter types:
The formats of random encounter that can be generated.

'combat'
'event'

d_game.py
The core module, in which the main game runs.
It starts by building the world with d_region.py and makes a PC from d_character.py.
